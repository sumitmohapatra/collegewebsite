About Us

Gandhi Institute For Technology (GIFT), Bhubaneswar ranked 29th in all India level and awarded as 'Band Excellent' institution in ATAL Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements (ARIIA) for the academic year 2021-22 by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India declared on 29th December 2021.

Top Engineering College in Odisha "GIFT Bhubaneswar" once again awarded Best Engineering College in Odisha for the year 2019" amongst the 'Top Engineering Colleges of Odisha' and "Best Principal for the year 2019" amongst all Engineering Colleges in Odisha by 'Indian Society For Technical Education', Odisha Section.

GIFT Engineering college ranked 2nd and 3rd position (among top engineering colleges in odisha) in Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements (ARIIA) 2019-2020 by MHRD, Govt. of India

Gandhi Institute For Technology (GIFT), Bhubaneswar (abbreviated GIFT or GIFT Bhubaneswar) is a top private engineering college in Odisha located in Bhubaneswar (ranked at no.1 position among smart cities in India, a Safe, Beautiful, Cosmopolitan Student City with unmatched pollution free environment near to World famous Shri Jagannath Temple in Puri and Konark Sun Temple), Odisha, India. The institute is affiliated to BPUT university in odisha and has been approved by AICTE. GIFT is a ISO 9001:2008 certified institute. Ranked No.1 among top 10 engineering colleges in Odisha and among top 3 engineering colleges in Bhubaneswar, the templecity and capital of Odisha and 27th position among top 100 engineering colleges in India, this most soughted after engineering college in the country is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), International Accreditation Organization (IAO), NAAC with 'A' Grade and also recognized and approved as a research center by 'Scientific & Industrial Research Organization (SIRO)' by Ministry of Science & Technology, Govt. of India. The institute was founded in 2007 by Balaram Panda Trust. The institute was awarded as the Most Promising Engineering College and Best Engineering College in Odisha by Governor of Odisha. In 2012 GIFT Engineering college became a remote center to IIT Bombay and IIT Kharagpur for conducting ISTE workshops. The institute provides Diploma, B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA and MBA courses and is the most demanded engineering and MBA college in Eastern India for top rank students because of the world class solar powered green infrastructure with academic excellence and 100% placement record. With students from 23 states of India, GIFT has one of the most vibrant campuses with state of art sporting infrastructure. One of the best Engineering and Management Institute in Eastern India, GIFT has the legacy of hosting the most prominent recruiters of the country. GIFT Business School has carved out a niche for itself because of its unique pedagogy of integrating industry required, industry taught and industry certified program within the regular curriculum. While Outside State students can't take admission in govt engineering colleges in Odisha, Being a top private engineering college in the state of Odisha, GIFT has become the top priority for outside students for taking admission in engineering colleges in Odisha as the tution fee of GIFT College is comparatively same as that of govt. colleges in Odisha and GIFT has provided stellar placements whereas some government colleges in Odisha have a very poor track record.
